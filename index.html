About Outer-Rim

Outer-Rim is a group of Millenials who met on a DayZ community server. Once that community fell apart, they decided to rebuld that community
on their own. 1.0 was a mess and a headache trying to chase bugs and problems on a constant. 1.25 was a test to see if something different would
work. We tried a map called Prypiat and it was a tragic failure as it caused conflict, and that, in turn, caused team members to seperate.
With a deep understanding behind the coding adn a new knowledge and perception 1.5 came along. 1.5 was a beefed up version of 1.0.
Then here we are today with 2.0. It is a 1.5 version with all the fixes neccesary to the 1.25 DayZ update. These trials and tests
could not have been done with out the Develpment team and Administrastor hard and fully dedicated work and partnership to bring you
the Outer-Rim you know today.
